What You Can Expect from K-9 Contain My Dog

I’m Steve Schwartz founder and head trainer and Containmydog. Keeping your dog safely contained in your yard is my business and passion. As pet owners, whether we adopt, purchase, or get a dog it’s amazing how quickly we fall in love with our ‘furkids’ and they become, in no time at all, full-fledged members of our family.

I started Containmydog because as a dog trainer I was disappointed in the training and level of service my clients received from the big name national companies. In starting Containmydog, I was driven to make sure my clients had what it took to keep their dogs safely contained and trained.

Containmydog installs the electric dog fences in Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Eastern Connecticut and Southern New Hampshire. We Guarantee containment success because we do the containment training. YOU Get to be the good guy with your dog.
